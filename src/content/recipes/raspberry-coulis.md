---
title: Raspberry Coulis
type: Dessert
serves: 350 ml batch
parts:
  - ingredients:
      - 250 g raspberry purée, defrosted
      - 50 g icing sugar
      - 50 ml lemon juice (1 lemon)
      - 50 ml water
    instructions:
      - step: 1
        description: In a mixing bowl add the icing sugar and the raspberry purée. Mix together.
      - step: 2
        description: Add the lemon juice and the 50 ml of water.
      - step: 3
        description: Sieve the mixture into a container. Store chilled.
storage: Store chilled. Shelf life 3 days.
---
