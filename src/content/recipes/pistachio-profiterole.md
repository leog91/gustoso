---
title: Pistachio Profiterole
img: https://i.imgur.com/H39Z10x.jpeg
parts:
  - name: Pistachio Patisserie
    ingredients:
      - 750ml milk
      - 6 eggs yolk
      - 150g Caster Sugar
      - 1/2 Vanilla pod (1.25ml)
      - 50g Flour
      - 50g Corn/Potato flour
      - 100g Pistachio Sauce
    instructions:
      - step: 1
        description: Bring the milk to a simmer with the vanilla
      - step: 2
        description: Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar until pale and thickened. Then whisk in the flour
      - step: 3
        description: Slowly mix the warm milk into the egg yolk mix
      - step: 4
        description: Transfer back to a pan and gently heat until thickened. Remove from the heat and transfer to a mixing bowl
      - step: 5
        description: Add the pistachio sauce then mix all together. Chill and store in a piping bag
  - name: Pistachio Profiterole
    ingredients:
      - 100g pistachio patisserie
      - 5 profiteroles
      - 1 scoop vanilla ice-cream
      - 20g pistachio sauce
      - 15ml pistachio chopped
    instructions:
      - step: 1
        description: Fill the profiterole with the patisserie
      - step: 2
        description: Pour the pistachio sauce over. Top with the chopped pistachio
---
