---
title: Panna Cotta
type: Dessert
serves: 7 portions
parts:
  - ingredients:
      - 6 gelatine leaves
      - 150 ml whole milk
      - 900 ml double cream
      - 1 x vanilla paste (5 ml)
      - 1 lemon, peelings only
      - 110 g caster sugar
    instructions:
      - step: 1
        description: Soften the gelatine leaves in cold water.
      - step: 2
        description: In a saucepan add the milk, cream and lemon peel. 
      - step: 3
        description: Add the vanilla paste. Heat the milk. DO NOT BOIL.
      - step: 4
        description: Remove from the heat. Add the sugar. Stir until dissolved. 
      - step: 5
        description: Drain the mixture. Squeeze the gelatine to remove excess water.
      - step: 6
        description: Mix until dissolved.
      - step: 7
        description: Blast chill (approx 30 mins).
      - step: 8
        description: Stir the half set panna cotta. Pour into moulds 1 cm from the top. Store chilled.
storage: Store chilled. Shelf life 3 days.
---
